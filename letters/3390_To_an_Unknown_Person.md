'

Have you heard the last variety of American Lion hunting -- writing to ask for a bit of one's dress to put into a literary quilt? I actually had such a request, and while I was laughing at it I came on a book of [Miss Alcott (Louisa May Alcott)](../people/1649.html)'s where the dear Jo -- after she had become a noted authoress -- has the same entreaty made to her.
