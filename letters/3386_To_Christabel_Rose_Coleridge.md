'

My dear [C C (Christabel Rose Coleridge)](../people/329.html) Do you want [*Campbell's Highland tales (Popular Tales of the West Highlands)*](../bibliography/general/201.html)? I dont think there is anything bearing on Arthur in them he was quite Cymric not Gaelic. I sent the two [Mags for young (*Magazine for the Young*)](../organizations/79.html) yesterday. Shall I write notices of [SPCK (Society for the Promotion of Christian Knowledge, The)](../organizations/40.html)'s books? They are not a good lot thus far as I have read, and there are two by Miss E Finnimore, [*the Postwoman*](../bibliography/general/199.html) and [*Uncle Isaac's will (Uncle Isaac's Money)*](../bibliography/general/200.html) that I am very angry with, they are about farmer folk of the rough cast sort, but with no token of principle or religion, true enough to nature unluckily, but not good to put out with SPCK stamp. I have written to [Mr Lathbury (Daniel Conner Lathbury)](../people/2159.html) to ask leave to say my say in the [Guardian (*Guardian, The*)](../organizations/75.html). [Phoebe Allen (Phoebe Anne Allen)](../people/25.html)'s [*mothers meeting story (May-Duke Blossoms: A story for Mothers' Meetings)*](../bibliography/general/202.html) gets into a complication of last fathers and sons not so good as last years and FE Beach who generally writes well, has made Ash Wednesday come after the 1st Sunday in Lent There must be a terrible want of censorship in the whole concern. We had a great [GFS (Girls' Friendly Society, The)](../organizations/23.html) course of sermon and meeting last week, and the [MU (Mothers' Union, The)](../organizations/31.html) comes in tomorrow and next day - Then the Hursley anniversary on Monday for which some of my people are coming, I lodging the women folk, [Miss Fin (Marion Finlaison)](../people/512.html) the men, having only too much room Very anxious to know what becomes of Preston and Mrs Jeune. I am very glad [Fanny (Frances Sophia Coleridge Patteson)](../people/1151.html) is coming to poor [Sophy (Sophia Martyn)](../people/974.html)

[Canon Wharton (Edgar Wharton)](../people/1675.html) preaches such a nice sermon on unconscious influence putting me so in mind of what has come round to me of [my Mother (Frances Mary Yonge)](../people/1606.html)'s effect on people, and bearing out my view that it is unconscious

I have written my Xmas GFS letter, it is rather stupid on the allegory of the voyage of life. [Miss Knight (Knight)](../people/1676.html) wants a New Years letter for GL - I mean to do the Century of Victory & progress with drawbacks

your affte C M Yonge

Do you get [N S (National Society, The)](../organizations/32.html) books?
