'

Dear [Mr Macmillan (Frederick Orridge Macmillan)](../people/942.html) Many thanks for your [cheque (CMY's literary earnings)](../organizations/160.html) which was an agreeable surprise to me. I hope the experiment of cheapness will be successful.

The great day at Hursley is to be the 24th October so if we could have a few of the presentation copies of [*John Keble's parishes (John Keble's Parishes: A History of Hursley and Otterbourne. By Charlotte M. Yonge, An Old Inhabitant.)*](../bibliography/cmy/247.html), it would be a great boon

Yours sincerely C M Yonge
