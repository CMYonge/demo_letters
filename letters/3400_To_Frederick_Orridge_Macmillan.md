'

Dear [Mr Macmillan (Frederick Orridge Macmillan)](../people/942.html) I am glad you see your way to publishing these books on the half profit system I send the [*Two Guardians (Two Guardians; or, Home in this World, The By the author of "Henrietta's Wish," "Kenneth," etc.)*](../bibliography/cmy/13.html) and [[*Henrietta's Wish (Henrietta's Wish, or, Domineering: A Tale. By the author of "Scenes and Characters". )*](../bibliography/cmy/11.html)]{.underline} shall follow, as soon as we can supply the first two pages which are gone from the only copy I can find

Yours truly C M Yonge
