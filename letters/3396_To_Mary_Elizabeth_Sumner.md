'

My dear [Mrs Sumner (Mary Elizabeth Sumner)](../people/1381.html) I hope you had the bundle of materials for the April [Mothers in Council (*Mothers in Council*)](../organizations/82.html) that I sent to Claremont, Rudleston, Manchester on the 31st January, for it is time [Wells Gardiner (Wells, Gardner)](../organizations/114.html) had them. If there is nothing to alter, perhaps you would leave them at his office, writing Mothers in Council on them. I only want to add a most excellent book of The Sunday School Institute [*'Christ in the Catechism' (Christ and the Catechism: The Church Catechism by 520 questions and answers explained)*](../bibliography/general/599.html) but that can be done in the proofs.

Here is [Mrs Bartram (Bartram)](../people/1668.html), Ramsgate Vicarage writing to ask leave for their Organist to set to music 'God Knows' out of our October number - I suppose there is no objection, but I do not now remember where it came from.

I shall wait to answer till I hear from you.

I have not had [Mrs Guslett (Guslett)](../people/1669.html)'s paper

Yours affectionately C M Yonge
