'

[Sir (Walter Besant)](../people/1652.html), I am moved to say something from my own personal knowledge in defence of rustic reading

I do not care whether this is inserted as a letter, but I should like the substance to appear, as I know it to be fact, and I think it is true of many other Hampshire villages[^1]

Yours truly C M Yonge

---

[^1]: It sounds as if this may have enclosed a letter to the <i>Author</i>, the organ of the [[other:44]Society of Authors].
