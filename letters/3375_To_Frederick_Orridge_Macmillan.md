'

Dear [Mr Macmillan (Frederick Orridge Macmillan)](../people/942.html) Herewith I send the MS of '[*John Keble's Parishes (John Keble's Parishes: A History of Hursley and Otterbourne. By Charlotte M. Yonge, An Old Inhabitant.)*](../bibliography/cmy/247.html)' I shall be glad to know what you think the best way of dealing with it and what you think about illustrations.

There should be a map of the parishes which are [locked]{.underline} together but I wait to get it drawn out till I hear about the size of the page

What would be advisable as to illustrations. We had thought of

Ruins of Merdon Castle Old house at Hursley Cup and other relics found in a deep well Great Seal of Richard Cromwell Old Church of Hursley Otterbourne Church Ampfield Church Hursley Church Portrait of [Sir William Heathcote (William Heathcote)](../people/669.html) & [Mr Keble (John Keble)](../people/798.html)

Would these be too expensive?

What would be the terms

If possible the book should be out by the middle of October, as a Jubilee of the Church's Consecration is to be kept on that day/24th

Yours sincerely C M Yonge
