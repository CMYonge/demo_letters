'Dear [Lady Leconfield (Constance Evelyn Wyndham)](../people/1664.html) As [Christabel Coleridge (Christabel Rose Coleridge)](../people/329.html) is here, I shall ask her to put in her autograph She is the remnant of my original [Goslings (Gosling Society, The)](../organizations/104.html) worked on as a [Spider (Spider Society, The)](../organizations/116.html) and now edits the [Packet (*Monthly Packet, The*)](../organizations/52.html), anxious to raise the tone, and bear it through the bad times for periodicals If you have read [*Waynflete*](../bibliography/general/205.html), the [*Main Chance (The Main Chance)*](../bibliography/general/206.html) and [*The Tender Mercies (The Tender Mercies of the Good)*](../bibliography/general/207.html), you will think her autograph worth having. She is the granddaughter of [S T Coleridge (Samuel Taylor Coleridge)](../people/1667.html) yours sincerely C M Yonge
