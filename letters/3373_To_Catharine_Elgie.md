'

My dear [Mrs Elgie (Catharine Elgie, later Elgee)](../people/475.html) There is a poor old surgeon of 80 for whom I have been voting for \[sic\]. If he is no more I will vote for [Annie Reynolds](../people/2192.html) as I always think Winchester has the first claim

[Caroline Ray](../people/2190.html), [Charles (Charles Ray)](../people/2111.html)'s eldest sister, who has spent her life in nursing invalid old gentlemen, and been well paid, takes Price's shop, as soon as his nephew can sell off the remains

Yours affly C M Yonge
