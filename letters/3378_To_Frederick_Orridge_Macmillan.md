'

Dear [Mr Macmillan (Frederick Orridge Macmillan)](../people/942.html) The Clerk of the Peace undertakes to ask the consent of [Mr Portal (Melville Portal)](../people/1196.html)and the Magistrates at the Quarter Sessions - so there is no doubt that this will be right.[^1]

Could the little wood cut of the Ampfield fountain (in the [*book (A Guide to the Village of Hursley, the Home of Keble, author of 'The Christian Year')*](../bibliography/general/124.html)) be inserted - And one view of the [old]{.underline} Hursley Church would be liked

Otherwise the list is very satisfactory.

That likeness of [Mr Keble (John Keble)](../people/798.html) has never been published, and would be much preferred if we can have it.

Otherwise the one in [*Lock's life (John Keble: A Biography)*](../bibliography/general/672.html)

Yours truly C M Yonge

---

[^1]: The letter refers to illustrations for *<i>John Keble's Parishes</i>*, which were to include a portrait of Sir William Heathcote hung in the County Hall in Winchester.
