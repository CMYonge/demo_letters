'

Dear [Mr Bullock (John Frederick Watkinson Bullock)](../people/221.html), I am glad you have succeeded at last in getting an opportunity for publishing[^1]

Certainly executors are very troublesome to deal with as they are not acting for themselves. I am sending an order paper for one person, a cousin, who I hope may have one

Yours truly C M Yonge

---

[^1]: CMY refers to Bullock's *<i>Daily Lections</i>* project, to which she was contributing.
